While there are numerous awesome software and tool lists available on the internet,
their primary objective is to list all the possible tools and software within certain
categories. Unfortunately, these lists can become overwhelming, and many of the entries
may not have been updated for an extended period of time.

I have curated a list of the most recent and actively maintained software and tools that I have personally discovered along the way. My aim is to provide a concise and focused list that will help you discover new, high-quality software and tools that are relevant to your needs.
I will mainly focus on including open source tools in my list. However, if there are any software or tools that are not open source but are available for free, I will still consider including them in my list.
